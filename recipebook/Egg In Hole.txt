Ingredients
1 Egg
A single piece of bread
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp red chilli powder
1/4 tsp black pepper
Coriander to sprinkle
 

Steps
In a piece of bread, punch a hole using a cup.
On a non-stick pan, spread some butter and let the bread toast. 
Crack the egg in the hole, add salt, chilli powder and black pepper on it as instructed. 
Let it cook. Once done, sprinkle some coriander leaves and serve hot.
