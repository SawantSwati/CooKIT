Ingredients 
1/8 Teaspoon Salt
1 cup Old-Fashioned Oats
2 Tablespoons Peanut Butter
2 Teaspoon Grounded Cinnamon
2 Tablespoons Honey 
 

Steps
Boil water and salt in a saucepan
Add the oats and stir in
Cook for 4-5 minutes on medium flame
Transfer to a bowl and add peanut butter, cinnamon and honey
